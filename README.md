### About me

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB), where I also served as a **Full-Time Teaching Assistant** in the CSE Department, supporting undergraduate students with coursework, labs, and academic guidance.

I'm currently a **Senior Software Engineer** at **ADN Technologies Limited**, where my work sits at the intersection of **fintech and IoT**:

- Developed **Echonex**, an in-house Spring Boot middleware platform for bKash payment speakers, featuring merchant management, merchant–device binding, and customized reporting dashboards
- Collaborated with **bKash** and led the development of an **IoT Soundbox** to optimize transaction processes and improve the overall user experience; also extended payment speaker integrations with other banks
- Led the development of financial software, managing a team and ensuring timely, high‑quality deliveries
- Collaborated with multiple banks - **Eastern Bank Ltd. (EBL), UCBL, DBBL, and IBBL** - to develop POS applications supporting major payment networks such as **Visa, MasterCard, Diners Club, and UPI**
- Deployed and managed an API‑based International Recharge Service for major telecom operators including **Grameenphone, Banglalink, Airtel, and Robi**
- Assisted in the development of an IoT hardware device for the **TRP project** at Bangladesh Satellite Company Limited (BSCL), enhancing satellite data collection and monitoring capabilities
- Built multiple in-house enterprise tools including **ADNT EMS**, **The Wave 24 EMS**, and **SRIM** (Service Repair & Inventory Management), as well as corporate websites and news portals

- 🎓 B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering, University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (CGPA 3.85)
- 🏫 Former Full-Time Teaching Assistant, ULAB CSE Department
- 📝 Published research in deep learning for freshwater fish species recognition, IoT home automation, and smart irrigation systems
- ⚡ Fun fact: I love to teach and learn new things - always exploring something new
- 👯 Open to collaborating on fintech, IoT, and applied ML projects
- 📫 Reach me at **shiam.prodhan.98@gmail.com**
